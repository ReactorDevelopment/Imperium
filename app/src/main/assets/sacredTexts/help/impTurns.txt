Turns|Each of your turns concists of three phases.  First you must choose how to spend your accumulated wealth.  Will you deploy battle ready troops to defend your land? Will you choose to develop your provinces to become shining centers of civilization?  Or will you choose to fortify your land to defend against your land hungry nehibors?  Next you can choose to attack enemy armies, conquering territory and expanding your domain.  At the end of your turn you may manuver your armies to nehiboring provinces to fit your strategy.  After you have spent all your available income, you can change the stage to the second " attack" stage by pressing the button in the bottom left corner of the screen.  You can press this button  at any time during the attack and transprot stages to move to the next.
