Gameplay|The name of the game is world domination.  You play as one nation amongst many, each vying for superiority.  Place troops down in the initial stage by clicking on the areas of land called provinces.  To see more information of a specific province, press and hold one of the provinces unitl the information panel slides out from the left.  For information on all the provinces, touch the arrow at the top center of the screen, this will show you the various map modes that you can view.  Each one colors the map differently to give you more information.  Clicking the arroe in the top right reveales a drawer which allows you to save your game, view game options, view your game's statistics, and quit the current game.
