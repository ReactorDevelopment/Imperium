Davastation|As armies clash, they scar the land with the horrors of war.  Crops become bepleted, villages are destroyed, and cities burn.  The level of destruction caused by this is represented by a province's devastation value.  The higher the devastation, the higher that provinces attrition, as well as lowering that province's exports to your treasury.  If the land is left in peace and allowed to rebuild however, it may become prosperious.  This prosperity increaces a provinces effective development, providing a safe haven for weary soilders.
