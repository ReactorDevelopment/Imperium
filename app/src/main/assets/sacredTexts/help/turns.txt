Turn Stages|Each of your turns concists of three phases, indicated in the bottom left corner of the screen in the status bar.  First you must choose where to deploy your troops so that they are ready for battle.  Next you can choose to attack enemy armies, conquering territory and expanding your domain.  At the end of your turn you may manuver your armies to nehiboring provinces to fit your strategy.  After you have spent all your available reinforcements, you can change the stage to the second " attack" stage by pressing the button in the bottom right corner of the screen.  You can press this button  at any time during the attack and transprort stages to move to the next.
